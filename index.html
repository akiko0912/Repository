Brownies

Ingredients

  • 20cm X 30cm tray
  • 250 grams of chocolate
  • 250 grams of butter
  • 300 grams of caster sugar
  • 150 grams of plain flour
  • 4 eggs

Step by step instruction

  1. Set the oven at 180℃

  2. Melt all of the butter and chocolate in a bowl

  3. In another bowl mix the 4 eggs and sugar

  4. Mix 1. and 2. together and finally add the flour

  5. Place the cooking paper on the tray. Pour it onto the tray.

  6. Put the tray in the 180℃ oven and wait for 10 minutes

  7. Poke the brownie with a think wooden stick. Make sure it is cooked. If not put the tray back into the oven and wait for 5 more minutes.

















Japanese Sweet and Chilli Spare Ribs

Ingredients

  • 250 ~ 300 grams of Spare Ribs
  • 1/2 Onions
  • 2 table spoon of soysauce
  • 1/2 or 2 table spoons of sugar
  • 2 table spoons of Japanese Sake
  • 50cc of water

Step by step instruction

  1. Poke the spare ribs with a fork several time. This will make the ribs to absorb the sauce faster later on

  2. Grate the 1/2 of onion

  3. Put the grated onion and the spare ribs in a bowl and mix it so the onions will equally stick onto the ribs

  4. Leave it for 15 minutes. Onions have the minerals which makes the ribs tender.

  5. After 15 minutes put them into a frying pan.

  6. Put soy sauce, sugar, Japanese Sake, and water into the frying pan as well and cook it with low heat for about 10 minutes.

  7. After 10 minutes turn it over and cook the other side.

  8. After cooking for total 20 minutes put the heat to the high and leave it to simmer.

  9. When there is no liquid or almost no liquid left, it's done.
