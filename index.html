Brownies

Ingredients

  • 20cm X 30cm tray
  • 250 grams of chocolate
  • 250 grams of butter
  • 300 grams of caster sugar
  • 150 grams of plain flour
  • 4 eggs

Step by step instruction

  1. Set the oven at 180℃.

  2. Melt all of the butter and chocolate in a bowl.

  3. In another bowl, mix the 4 eggs and the sugar.

  4. Mix 1. and 2. together and finally add the flour.

  5. Line a baking tray with baking paper. Pour the mixture onto the tray.

  6. Put the tray in the oven and leave for 10 minutes.

  7. Poke the brownie with the end of a knife to ensure it is cooked. If the mixture is still runny, put the tray back into the oven and wait for 5 more minutes. Repeat this until the brownies are at the desired consistency.



Japanese Sweet and Chilli Spare Ribs

Ingredients

  • 250 ~ 300 grams of Spare Ribs
  • 1/2 Onions
  • 2 table spoon of soysauce
  • 1/2 or 2 table spoons of sugar
  • 2 table spoons of Japanese Sake
  • 50cc of water

Step by step instruction

  1. Poke the spare ribs with a fork several times. This will help the ribs to absorb the sauce faster later on

  2. Grate the onion.

  3. Put the grated onion and the spare ribs in a bowl and mix until the onions are of an equal consistency over the ribs.

  4. Leave it for 15 minutes. Onions have the minerals which makes the ribs tender.

  5. After 15 minutes, put the ribs into a frying pan.

  6. Add the soy sauce, sugar, Japanese Sake, and water into the frying pan and fry on a low heat for about 10 minutes.

  7. Turn the ribs over after 10 minutes until both sides are cooked to an equal extent.

  8. After cooking for total 20 minutes, increase the heat to the highest setting and leave it to simmer.

  9. When there is no liquid or almost no liquid left, it's done.
